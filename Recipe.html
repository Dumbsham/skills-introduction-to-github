NORTH INDIAN

CHOLE BHATURE

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
WHITE GRAM 1 cup
tomato 3
green chilli 4
ginger 100gm/2inch
green coriander 250gm
vegetable oil 2tbsp
tej patta 2
elaichi 2pcs
clove 2
cinnamon 1 inch
spices as per requirement
Method

First rinse 1 cup white chickpeas a couple of times in water. Then soak them in enough water overnight or for 7 to 8 hours.

To prepare chole: In a pan add oil, bay leaf, cinnamon, cumin seeds, cloves, whole pepper corns, green and black cardamom.

  1. After it browns, add chopped onions and saute. Add chopped ginger and garlic.
  2. Now, add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, asafoetida and salt. Fry the ingredients together.
  3. For de-glazing the pan, add a little water.
  4. Now, add the chole (soaked overnight and pressure cooked) to the masala.
  5. After stirring well, add tomatoes, a little sugar and salt to the chole.
  6. Now, add ajwain, chopped green chilies and water for the base.
  7. To get the color in the chole, add a tea bag to the masala.
  8. Simmer the chole gently for an hour and cover it.
  9. Add lime juice and a dollop of butter to it.
  10. Garnish the chole with coriander and butter and serve them hot with bhaturas.

    11. To prepare the bhaturas:

  11. Knead the whole wheat flour, maida and salt together with adequate amount of water.
  12. Sprinkle the yeast on top. Leave it for 2-3 hours for the yeast to work.
  13. Divide it into equal portions. Roll out in an oval or round shape.
  14. Deep fry till golden brown, like a puri. Serve and enjoy!
